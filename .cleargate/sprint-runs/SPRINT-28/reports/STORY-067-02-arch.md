After tightening, items with leftover `Done`/`Verified` status would NOT be skipped — they'd be candidates for the reconciler's auto-flip or halt path. Since no such items exist (point 1), no behaviour change occurs on first run. Future regressions (a Dev hand-typing `status: Done`) would now be CAUGHT by the reconciler instead of silently treated as terminal — this is the desired post-CR-067 behaviour.
